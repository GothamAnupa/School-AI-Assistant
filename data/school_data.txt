Narayana Public School - Academic Data (2025-2026)
1. Telugu Syllabus
Poetry (పద్య భాగం):
Matrubhavana (మాతృభావన)
Sataka Madhurima (శతక మధురిమ)
Samudralanghanam (సముద్రలంఘనం)
Bhiksha (భిక్ష)
Prose (గద్య భాగం):
Jaanapaduni Jaabu (జానపదుని జాబు)
Dhanyudu (ధన్యుడు)
Maa Prayatnam (మా ప్రయత్నం)
Gorantha Deepalu (గోరంత దీపాలు)
Non-Detailed (ఉపవాచకం):
Ramayanam: Bala Kaandam, Ayodhya Kaandam, Aranya Kaandam, Kishkindha Kaandam, Sundara Kaandam, Yuddha Kandam.
Grammar (వ్యాకరణం):
Sandhulu: Savarna Deergha, Guna, Vriddhi, Yanadesha, Telugu Sandhulu (Akaara, Ikaara, Ukaara).
Samaasaalu: Dwandva, Dvigu, Bahuvreehi, Rupaka.
Chandassu: Utpalamala, Champakamala, Mattebham, Shardulam.
Alankaralu: Upama, Atisayokti, Arthantaranyasa.
Others: Paryaaya Padaalu, Prakriti-Vikritulu, Jateeyaalu (Idioms).

Grammar & Writing:
Grammar: Sandhi, Samas (Tatpurush, Dwandva, Bahuvrihi), Alankar (Anupras, Upma, Rupak), Vakya Parivartan (Sentence Transformation), Muhavare (Idioms), and Lokoktiyan.
Writing Skills: Formal/Informal Letter Writing, Essay Writing (Nibandh), and Poster Preparation.
3. English Syllabus
First Flight (Prose): A Letter to God, Nelson Mandela: Long Walk to Freedom, Two Stories about Flying, From the Diary of Anne Frank, Glimpses of India, Mijbil the Otter, Madam Rides the Bus, The Sermon at Benares, The Proposal.
First Flight (Poetry): Dust of Snow, Fire and Ice, A Tiger in the Zoo, How to Tell Wild Animals, The Ball Poem, Amanda!, The Trees, Fog, The Tale of Custard the Dragon, For Anne Gregory.
Footprints Without Feet: A Triumph of Surgery, The Thief's Story, The Midnight Visitor, A Question of Trust, Footprints Without Feet, The Making of a Scientist, The Necklace, Bholi, The Book That Saved the Earth.
Grammar & Writing: Tenses, Modals, Subject-Verb Concord, Reported Speech, Determiners; Letter Writing (Formal), Analytical Paragraphs.
4. Mathematics Syllabus
Number Systems: Real Numbers.
Algebra: Polynomials, Pair of Linear Equations in 2 Variables, Quadratic Equations, Arithmetic Progressions (AP).
Geometry: Triangles, Circles.
Trigonometry: Introduction to Trigonometry, Trigonometric Identities, Heights and Distances.
Mensuration: Areas Related to Circles, Surface Areas and Volumes.
Statistics & Probability: Mean, Median, Mode of Grouped Data, Probability Theory.
5. Science Syllabus
Chemistry: Chemical Reactions & Equations, Acids, Bases & Salts, Metals & Non-metals, Carbon & its Compounds.
Biology: Life Processes (Nutrition, Respiration, Transport, Excretion), Control & Coordination, How do Organisms Reproduce?, Heredity and Evolution.
Physics: Light: Reflection & Refraction, The Human Eye & Colorful World, Electricity, Magnetic Effects of Electric Current.
Natural Resources: Our Environment (Food chains, Ozone depletion, Waste management).
6. Social Studies Syllabus
History: Rise of Nationalism in Europe, Nationalism in India, The Making of a Global World, Print Culture & The Modern World.
Geography: Resources & Development, Forest & Wildlife, Water Resources, Agriculture, Minerals & Energy, Manufacturing Industries, Lifelines of National Economy.
Political Science: Power Sharing, Federalism, Gender, Religion & Caste, Political Parties, Outcomes of Democracy.
Economics: Development, Sectors of the Indian Economy, Money & Credit, Globalization & The Indian Economy.
